Safety Guidelines

Critical safety instructions for working with chemical reagents

Important Warning

This system is for trained professionals only. Chemical reagents are dangerous and must be handled with extreme caution in an appropriate laboratory environment.

Basic Safety Rules

1

Always use chemical-resistant gloves

2

Ensure excellent ventilation or use a fume hood

3

Do not touch reagents directly - may cause severe burns

4

Keep reagents away from children and flammable materials

5

Follow safe disposal procedures for chemical waste

6

These tests are for trained professionals only in a safe laboratory environment

Emergency Procedures

!

In case of skin contact: Immediately wash with cold water for 15 minutes

!

In case of eye contact: Rinse with running water for 15 minutes and seek medical help

!

In case of inhalation: Move to fresh air immediately

!

In case of ingestion: Do not induce vomiting and seek medical help immediately

Required Equipment

Personal Protective Equipment

  • • Chemical-resistant gloves
  • • Safety goggles
  • • Lab coat
  • • Closed-toe shoes

Laboratory Equipment

  • • Fume hood
  • • Emergency eyewash station
  • • Safety shower
  • • Fire extinguisher
